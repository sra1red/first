Chess is mind game which is popular in most of the countries.
It is played by 2 players.
Description:
Chess board has 8*8 squares which are black and white square boxes.
The two players need to choose either black or white side.
There are 16 white and black piecies for each empire and the 2 players need to fight with each other using their empires.
For each empire their is a King, Minister, two horses, two camels, two elephants and 8 soldiers.
If king is captured then that empire will loose and opponent wins the battle.
